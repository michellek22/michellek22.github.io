PANCAKES

About Pancakes

Pancakes are one of the most beloved and versatile breakfast dishes around the world. Soft, warm, and endlessly customizable, they're often enjoyed with a drizzle of syrup, a pat of butter, fresh fruits, or even whipped cream. From lazy Sunday mornings to quick weekday treats, pancakes have become a breakfast staple in many households.

Surprisingly, pancakes have been around for thousands of years, with the word “pancake” first appearing in English during the 15th century. Over time, they've evolved across cultures—but their comforting charm has remained the same.

The Perfect Pancake Recipe

Whether you're cooking for yourself, your family, or trying to impress someone special, a batch of fluffy pancakes is always a good idea. This easy recipe is quick, beginner-friendly, and guaranteed to deliver soft, golden pancakes every time.

List of Ingredients

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 tablespoons sugar
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 3/4 cup milk
  • 1 egg
  • 2 tablespoons melted butter
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Instructions

  1. Mix the flour, sugar, baking powder, baking soda, and salt.
  2. In another bowl, whisk the milk, egg, melted butter, and vanilla.
  3. Combine wet and dry ingredients.
  4. Heat a pan and lightly grease it.
  5. Pour 1/4 cup of batter for each pancake.
  6. Cook until bubbles form, flip, and cook the other side.
  7. Serve warm with syrup or toppings.

Tips for Perfect Pancakes

Don't overmix your batter! Let it rest 5 minutes before cooking for fluffier results.
